Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

What we collect: nothing. Coopr has no accounts, no analytics SDKs, no ads, and no tracking. We never see your flock records, egg logs, expenses, or sales.

Where your data lives. All data is stored on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is also stored in your personal iCloud account, protected by Apple's iCloud security. We have no access to it.

Purchases. Coopr Plus purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive no personal information about you.

Export. You can export your data as CSV at any time from the More tab.

Children. Coopr does not collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes. If this policy ever changes, the update will appear here with a new date.
